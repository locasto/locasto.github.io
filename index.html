Bio

Michael E. Locasto, Ph.D., is a Principal Computer Scientist at SRI's New York City cyberanalytics research hub and a member of the Infrastructure Security Group. He was previously a tenured Associate Professor at the University of Calgary, where he directed the Trustworthy Systems Group and conducted research in trustworthy systems, cooperative security mechanisms, and software security.

Dr. Locasto is the PI for SRI's Threat Intelligence for Grid Recovery (TIGR) project under the DARPA RADICS program, which focuses on finding, characterizing, and removing malware in power grid equipment. He co-leads SRI's Internet of Things Security and Privacy Center.

Dr. Locasto has co-authored over 70 publications in the field of computer security, and he holds 13 patents related to software security and intrusion detection. He has a PhD, MPhil, and MSc in CS from Columbia University and graduated summa cum laude from The College of New Jersey (TCNJ).
